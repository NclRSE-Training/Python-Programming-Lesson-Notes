## 5.2 Understanding the loops

Given the following loop:
```python
word = 'oxygen'
for char in word:
  print(char)
```

How many times is the body of the loop executed?

1.  3 times
1.  4 times
1.  5 times
1.  6 times

<details>
  <summary>
  Solution
  </summary>

  The body of the loop is executed 6 times.
